About Us

Feex is a global leading payments and risk management company focused on providing the most advanced payment technology, to ensure merchants can access multiple payment solutions over multiple regions so consumers can make payment anywhere in the world.

Our Vision

Our Vision at FeeX is to lead and support global gambling and managed risk operators by providing efficient solutions and functionality for their day-to-day operations, enabling them to maximize growth potential. We offer a specialized payments platform tailored to the gambling sector, encompassing all channels and connecting to a global network of local payment solutions specific to each market.

With Feex, operators gain the capability to integrate new customer payment methods and third-party service providers seamlessly. Our platform allows defining and implementing rules and workflows for efficient utilization, setting up real-time customer communications, and monitoring the entire system to ensure optimal performance. Rest assured, we maintain full compliance with PCI DSS Level 1 (soon).

Why Choose Feex

Choosing Feex means choosing a reliable partner that understands the complexities of international commerce. Our dedication to security, speed, and efficiency allows businesses to focus on growth, confident that their payment processes are in safe hands.
