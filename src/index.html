Leading Technology Solutions

Feex specializes in the gambling and managed risk sectors, utilizing leading technology to seamlessly connect operators to global payment solutions. Our expertise in managing payment risk allows us to develop cutting-edge solutions, simplifying the payment process. Beyond that, our core mission revolves around connecting people, enabling buyers and sellers to interact effortlessly on a global scale

The FeeX Platform

The platform originated from a team of skilled payment software professionals boasting more than 5 years of expertise. Back in 2013, our platform was established with a specific focus on the managed risk sector, establishing connections with numerous European banks.

Our platform's strength lies not only in its well-established connections but also in the rich functionality and features it offers. These elements significantly enhance the value of your payment processing experience. At Feex, we are dedicated to continuously improving and expanding our services to provide top-notch payment processing solutions to our valued clients.

Our Vision

Our Vision at FeeX is to lead and support global gambling and managed risk operators by providing efficient solutions and functionality for their day-to-day operations, enabling them to maximize growth potential. We offer a specialized payments platform tailored to the gambling sector, encompassing all channels and connecting to a global network of local payment solutions specific to each market.

With Feex, operators gain the capability to integrate new customer payment methods and third-party service providers seamlessly. Our platform allows defining and implementing rules and workflows for efficient utilization, setting up real-time customer communications, and monitoring the entire system to ensure optimal performance. Rest assured, we maintain full compliance with PCI DSS Level 1 (soon).

Why Choose Feex

Choosing Feex means choosing a reliable partner that understands the complexities of international commerce. Our dedication to security, speed, and efficiency allows businesses to focus on growth, confident that their payment processes are in safe hands.
